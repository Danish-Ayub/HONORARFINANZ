Our company history

As early as 2009, we recognized that a business relationship based on partnership without conflicts of interest in the area of ​​finance is only possible through an independent and commission-free service. Even then, investors were given access to the capital markets under the company name "AVENTUS FINANCE" via inexpensive index funds and net products, i.e. commission-free financial products.

In 2013, the company was renamed Honorarfinanz AG with the aim of offering competent and professional advice to investors and consumers nationwide.

With the introduction of the law on fee-based investment advice in 2014, a new job profile was created, which guarantees consumers the legal security of independent, commission-free investment advice. Our activity was thus “officially” recognised.

On January 1st, 2016, Honorarfinanz AG was licensed by the German Bundesbank and BaFin (Federal Financial Supervisory Authority) with admission to the institute for fee-based investment advice.

In the future, nationwide qualified fee-based investment advisors will act as investor coaches in order to give investors access to individual and cost-efficient investment concepts.
