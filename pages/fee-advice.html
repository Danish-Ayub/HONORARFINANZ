What exactly do we do as an independent fee consultant?

Many people lack a comprehensive overview of their finances. A listing of completed products without a clear “roadmap” for the future is of little use. Our independent fee-based advice goes far beyond that, because only professional advice that works independently of product recommendations and also takes tax aspects into account gives clear information about where you actually stand with your finances.

As a fee and investment advisor, we take care of your money. Our liquidity and wealth planning reflects your entire individual financial situation. It captures all income and expenses as well as all your financial products, assets and liabilities. Financial planning simulates the development of all finances up to retirement and beyond. After completing this comprehensive investigation, your independent fee-based advisor will work with you to set the course for future financial decisions.
