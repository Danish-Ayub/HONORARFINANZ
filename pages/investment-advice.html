Why do we only use ETFs? Because your money is at stake!
Why ETFs

If you opt for independent fee-based investment advice from Honorarfinanz AG, ETFs, i.e. exchange-traded index funds, will be recommended for investment without exception. ETFs combine the advantages of stocks and funds in one financial product and also offer investors a number of advantages. Since our advice is always characterized by being free of commission and being independent, it makes sense that we only offer our clients exchange-traded index funds.

The advantages of ETFs are obvious:

  • Low costs:

    Because ETFs are passively managed.

  • Broad diversification:

    An entire asset class can be covered with just one transaction.

  • Transparency with high liquidity:

    ETFs are traded on stock exchanges, which allows a daily overview of performance and costs.

  • Maximum security:

    As special assets, ETFs are also protected in the event of insolvency.
