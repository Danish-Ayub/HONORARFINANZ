What our independent advice on retirement provision has in store for you

We work with you to calculate how you can make your dreams come true in old age. When it comes to retirement planning, a number of questions arise that you can clarify with your fee-based advisor during a retirement planning consultation. With our retirement planning, we simulate your future in old age.

Is your money and your basic pension sufficient to meet your standard of living requirements?
How can a supply gap that arises from the statutory pension insurance alone be closed? Which investment strategy for retirement is right for you? We look into all these questions in the fee-based advice on old-age provision - of course completely neutral.

The simulation is carried out with various life expectancy and inflation scenarios. Based on this, a possible supply gap is determined. As a result, solutions to close the pension gap are calculated based on personal requirements and tax requirements and simulated according to profitability.

The result of the advice on old-age provision, which is completely independent, is an individual and precise financial plan for the implementation of your dreams and wishes.
Find out what type of investment you are and which products are suitable for you. Protect yourself for old age and look back on high returns through commission-free and low-cost products.

Our 4 performance promises for you

We don't make empty promises. As an independent financial service provider, we guarantee you the following results:

Guaranteed Independence.

As one of only 17 BaFin-certified institutes in Germany, we are subject to the absolute ban on accepting commissions. Fee instead of commission means for you: 100% independent investment advice in your interest.

The Most Efficient Solutions.

Through us, you get access to the most efficient and cost-effective investment products of our time. As an investor, you benefit from full participation in the returns - without high costs and expensive management.

Guaranteed More Return.

By lowering product costs by up to 50% and using a forecast-free investment strategy, we increase the return on your investment by up to 100%. You participate in the full power of the capital markets and thus achieve your financial goals.

A Positive Investment Experience.

With our fee-based advice and our investor coaching, you will have fun investing in the future. Regardless of banks and insurance companies, you will have great, positive experiences. Advice just the way you always wanted it.

The perfect strategy for every investor

All strategies of Honorarfinanz AG are based on the "world portfolio approach" and are aimed at people who are interested in long-term investments. As an investor, you have the choice between three performance-oriented portfolios that you can use as a guide. Alternatively, if you wish, you can have your personal fee-based investment advisor put together a very individual portfolio for you.

Which investor are you? Are you more risk averse or risk tolerant? Every setting has its justification. Test your willingness to take risks now.

All-Weather Portfolio

With this strategy, you secure your assets in the long term and achieve positive returns in almost every market situation. The strategy for profit-oriented investors.

Sustainability Portfolio

Are ethical, social and ecological aspects important to you as an investor? You want to achieve as broad an impact as possible on people and the environment. Then this strategy is for you.

Scientific Portfolio

Would you like to consciously control your risk while consistently achieving the highest returns on the global capital markets? With this strategy you achieve the highest diversification and the maximum returns on the capital markets.

Individual Portfolio

As individual as you and tailored to your goals. The whole thing is precisely tailored to your personal risk profile. With this strategy, we will construct your desired portfolio for you. Ideal for professional investors and self-deciders.
