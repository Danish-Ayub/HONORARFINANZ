Independent advice on retirement provision

With us, the first step in retirement planning has nothing to do with financial products. First of all, the independent advice on retirement provision is all about you. What dreams and wishes do you have that you would like to realize later in old age and what standard of living are you striving for?

Decide for yourself what goals you want to achieve when you retire
Financial security

I want to achieve financial security in old age. Without having to work, I would like to be able to completely cover my private expenses. My entire livelihood should be covered by passive income.

Financial Vitality

In addition to financial security, I would also like to gain financial vitality and be able to afford the nice things in life in old age. Travel, going to restaurants and a little luxury are important to me, even in old age.

Financial independence

I want to achieve financial independence and freedom. I don't want to have to work anymore for the lifestyle I have today. I want to live where I want and do what I want.

What our independent advice on retirement provision has in store for you

We work with you to calculate how you can make your dreams come true in old age. When it comes to retirement planning, a number of questions arise that you can clarify with your fee-based advisor during a retirement planning consultation. With our retirement planning, we simulate your future in old age.

Is your money and your basic pension sufficient to meet your standard of living requirements?
How can a supply gap that arises from the statutory pension insurance alone be closed? Which investment strategy for retirement is right for you? We look into all these questions in the fee-based advice on old-age provision - of course completely neutral.

The simulation is carried out with various life expectancy and inflation scenarios. Based on this, a possible supply gap is determined. As a result, solutions to close the pension gap are calculated based on personal requirements and tax requirements and simulated according to profitability.

The result of the advice on old-age provision, which is completely independent, is an individual and precise financial plan for the implementation of your dreams and wishes.
Find out what type of investment you are and which products are suitable for you. Protect yourself for old age and look back on high returns through commission-free and low-cost products.
