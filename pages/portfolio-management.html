How does portfolio management work?

Portfolio management has the task of compiling and managing a portfolio. The composition of a portfolio depends on the ideas of the investor. You decide for yourself which of the portfolio strategies we propose suits you. Honorarfinanz AG specializes in passive investment strategies using Exchange Traded Funds (ETF). In addition to ETFs and ETCs, our broad investment portfolio also offers you special asset class index funds, giving investors a wide range of options to choose from. With these instruments, you can invest even more precisely and efficiently in the global capital markets with the established portfolios.

The portfolio management exclusively pursues your individual interests and is completely independent of product providers. You can also implement the strategies yourself via your direct bank. With the help of our portfolio strategies, you invest efficiently with the buy and hold approach. This approach is designed to buy securities, shares and other investments and to hold them for as long as possible.

Our portfolio strategies

Would you like to set up an ETC, asset class or ETF portfolio? We take over all portfolio analysis strategies for you. There are different approaches to managing your finances.

All-Weather Portfolio

The idea of ​​the all-weather portfolio is based on a fixed risk structure. Asset classes are not evaluated based on their return, but based on their risk.With the help of this investment strategy, you, your investment portfolio and your return are well prepared for all market phases.

Invest sustainably

In the so-called sustainability portfolio, it is the task of portfolio management to invest in sustainable and ecological companies. In addition to considering the risks of the investments, the ethical, social and ecological point of view for the composition of the portfolio strategies is considered here.

Scientific Portfolio

This approach to portfolio management aims to achieve the highest returns over the long term based on your personally assembled portfolio. The scientific portfolio is suitable for investors who want to consciously control the risk of the projects.

Individual portfolio strategy

The personal goals and ideas of the investor flow into an individual investment strategy. This portfolio strategy is particularly suitable for those who make their own decisions and professional investors who would then like to take over the portfolio management themselves.
